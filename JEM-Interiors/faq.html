When do I need an Interior Designer?

If you’re building a new home or office, an interior designer can help you create a functional, stylish, and cohesive layout.

Why do I need an Interior Designer?

An interior designer can transform your space by combining creativity, functionality, and expertise.

Why do you have a design fee and purchasing fee on the product?

The design fee and purchasing fee cover different aspects of the services we provide to ensure a seamless and high-quality experience.

What should I have at our first meeting for my project?

A clear idea of the space(s) you want to work on and your goals for the project.
Floor plans, if available, or rough measurements of the space.

How much will my interior design project cost?

High-end materials, custom furniture, or intricate designs may increase costs.

Minimalist or simple designs often have a lower price point.

How long will it take to to design and build my project?

A single room redesign typically takes a few weeks, while larger spaces or entire homes can take several months.
