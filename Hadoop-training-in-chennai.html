Hadoop Course Program Features

Description

Unlock the power of big data with our Hadoop Training at Skifter Technology. This course covers the essential concepts of Hadoop, including HDFS (Hadoop Distributed File System), MapReduce, and YARN (Yet Another Resource Negotiator). You’ll learn how to process and analyze large datasets using Hadoop's powerful ecosystem, including tools like Hive, Pig, and HBase. With hands-on projects and real-world use cases, you’ll gain the skills needed to manage, store, and analyze big data at scale. Join us and step into the world of big data analytics with Hadoop!
