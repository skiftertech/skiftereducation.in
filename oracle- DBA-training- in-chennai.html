Description

Learn DBA Course with Skifter Education to gain in-depth knowledge of Oracle DBA Architecture, database structures, memory and process architecture, security, schema objects, data backup, recovery and various concepts. We provide Oracle DBA training to improve your primary skills in all the major database platforms, including 10g, 11g, and 12c. Our Oracle DBA training in Chennai provide all types of DBA learning programs including systems, development and application.

This Oracle DBA course (10g, 11g and 12c) includes fundamental to advanced level concepts designed to help you get OCA and OCP Certifications. Our Oracle DBA trainers are industry experts having hands-on real-time project knowledge.
